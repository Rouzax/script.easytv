# Episode Export

> **Configured under** **Settings → Advanced → Tools → Export episodes to folder...**

Copy your "next episode" files to another location. Useful for offline viewing on trips, transferring to a portable device, or quick backups.

---

## How It Works

1. Select episodes (in Browse Mode) or trigger a full export (from Settings)
2. Choose a destination folder
3. EasyTV copies the actual video files

---

## Using the Exporter

### From Browse Mode

1. Open Browse Mode (episode list)
2. Open the context menu (`C` or menu button)
3. Toggle on **Multi-Select**
4. Select the episodes you want to export
5. Open the context menu again and choose **Export Selection**
6. Pick a destination folder
7. Files are copied; progress is shown

### From Settings

1. Go to **Settings → Advanced → Tools**
2. Click **Export episodes to folder...**
3. Pick a destination folder
4. EasyTV exports the current next episode for every show it tracks (one episode per show)

---

## Export Details

- Copies the actual video files. No shortcuts or symlinks.
- Preserves original filenames.
- Shows progress during the transfer.
- Reports any failures at the end.

---

## Use Cases

| Scenario | Workflow |
|----------|----------|
| **Trip preparation** | Export a few episodes from multiple shows |
| **Device transfer** | Export to USB drive, copy to tablet |
| **Backup** | Periodically export next episodes to NAS |
| **Sharing** | Export specific episodes for someone |

---

## Limitations

- Only video files are copied. Subtitles, NFO files, and extras are not included.
- Large files take time to copy.
- The destination needs enough free space.
- Network destinations are slower than local ones.
